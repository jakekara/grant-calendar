Municipal aid: When the checks go out

Municipal grants are scheduled to go out in portions throughout the year. Without a budget, the governor's executive order dictates funding levels of each grant. This calendar shows when portions of each grant are scheduled to be paid to towns. Choose your town to see how funding would change for certain grants under the governor's plan.
